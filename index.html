Integrations

Connects to everything

Your counters don't live in isolation. They talk to your smart home, your automation apps, your web services, and even physical NFC tags.

Webhooks

Fire HTTP requests (GET, POST, PUT, PATCH, DELETE) with custom headers, authentication, JSON payloads, and template variables. Connect to Home Assistant, Google Sheets, Zapier, Make, or any REST API.

Home Assistant Google Sheets Zapier Any REST API

Broadcast Intents

Send Android broadcast intents that Tasker, Automate, and MacroDroid can catch. Turn your counters into triggers for any phone automation — control Bluetooth, send SMS, launch apps, and more.

Tasker Automate MacroDroid

NFC Tags

Write counter actions to physical NFC tags. Tap your phone on a sticker to increment, decrement, or reset a counter — no app opening needed. Stick them on your water bottle, gym bag, or front door.

NDEF Write Tap to Count Zero Setup

Telegram

Send automatic messages to any Telegram chat when a counter event fires. Custom message templates with dynamic variables like counter value, title, and timestamp.

Bot API Custom Templates Real-time
